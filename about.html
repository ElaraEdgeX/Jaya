Get to Know More About Jaya

Journey towards Therapist:
Jaya Banala is a dedicated expert in personal empowerment and mindset transformation. With years of experience in personal development and wellness, she has devoted her career to helping others unlock their potential and live a life of abundance. As a sought-after speaker and workshop leader, Jaya has inspired countless individuals to shift their perspectives and embrace the possibilities that life offers. When not writing or speaking, Jaya enjoys meditating and researching innovative ways to fuel her commitment to living a fulfilling and balanced life.

Jaya believes that everyone has the power to create the life they desire and is ready to help you on your journey toward a more prosperous and joyful soul.

Her unique approach combines scientific principles with holistic practices, making self-love accessible and achievable for everyone. Through workshops, coaching sessions, and now this comprehensive guide, she has empowered countless individuals to embrace their authentic selves and lead happier, more fulfilling lives.

Jaya believes in the power of gratitude, mindfulness, and self-compassion. She lives in Birmingham, UK with her very supportive husband, their beautiful daughter, and their loving dog, Zeke.

Her spiritual journey began when she moved to the UK alone, leaving her four-year-old child and husband behind in pursuit of a brighter future for her family. She has been associated with various faiths such as Brahma Kumaris, ISKCON, and the Isha Foundation. Ultimately, she discovered and experienced God as a form of energy, realising that traditional religion can sometimes restrict our ability to fully connect with this immense source of power.
